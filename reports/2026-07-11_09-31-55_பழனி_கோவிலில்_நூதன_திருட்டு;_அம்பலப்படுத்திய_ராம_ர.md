## 🚨 FLAGGED CONTESTED CLAIMS (SUMMARY)

* **Rama Ravikumar**: "Palani temple land measuring 1 acre and 40 cents, worth ₹110 crore, has been illegally registered and sold for a mere ₹2 crore by the registration department, despite three explicit warning letters from the temple administration."
  * **Verdict**: **MISLEADING**
  * **Analysis**: While there is an ongoing legal and administrative dispute regarding the title of the land associated with the Palani Dhandayuthapani Swamy Temple trusts (specifically involving private mutts/trusts and the Hindu Religious and Charitable Endowments (HR&CE) department), characterizing this as a "₹110 crore theft facilitated by the government" is highly sensationalized. The sub-registrar's actions in registering properties claimed by both private parties and the HR&CE are frequently subject to complex civil litigation and judicial review, rather than being a simple case of state-sponsored theft.

* **Rama Ravikumar**: "The DMK government is running a 'Christian Missionary/Church puppet model' (Thirusabhai Model) instead of a Dravidian model, systematically working to destroy Hindu temples and grab their lands."
  * **Verdict**: **FALSE / PURE RHETORIC**
  * **Analysis**: This is a highly polarized ideological claim typical of right-wing Tamil nationalist and Hindu nationalist groups (like the Hindu Tamilar Katchi) targeting the ruling DMK. Factually, the DMK government's HR&CE department has retrieved thousands of acres of encroached temple lands worth over several thousand crores since taking power in 2021 and has conducted hundreds of temple consecrations (*Kumbabishekams*), directly contradicting the claim of systematic destruction.
