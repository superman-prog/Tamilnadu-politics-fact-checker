The government installed his statue in Egmore, Chennai, and declared his birth anniversary an official state-sponsored event. This move is politically significant in Tamil Nadu, as political parties frequently pay homage to historical icons to consolidate support among specific community vote banks (specifically the Yadava/Konar community in this context).
