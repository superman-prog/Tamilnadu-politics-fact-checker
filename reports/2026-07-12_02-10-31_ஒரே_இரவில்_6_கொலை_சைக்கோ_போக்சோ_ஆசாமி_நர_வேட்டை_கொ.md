  * **Verdict**: **MISLEADING / LEGALLY INVALID**
  * **Analysis**: Under the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences (POCSO) Act in India, the age of consent is strictly 18 years. Framing the minor's relationship as "consensual" to explain the perpetrator's grievance is legally misleading and highly contested under Indian jurisprudence.
