## 🚨 FLAGGED CONTESTED CLAIMS (SUMMARY)

* **CTR Nirmal Kumar**: "TTV Dhinakaran is a leader who abandoned his party cadres and left them stranded on the streets."
  * **Verdict**: **MISLEADING**
  * **Analysis**: This statement is a highly charged political rhetoric aimed at delegitimizing TTV Dhinakaran (leader of the Amma Makkal Munnetra Kazhagam - AMMK) and consolidating the AIADMK's position as the sole custodian of the legacy of MGR and Jayalalithaa. While it is factually true that AMMK has suffered massive defections, loss of electoral deposits, and a severe decline in political relevance since its peak in 2017–2019—forcing many grassroots workers to return to the AIADMK or join the BJP—characterizing this as Dhinakaran "abandoning" them is a subjective political attack. The exodus of cadres is primarily driven by systemic electoral losses and the pragmatic survival instincts of local politicians rather than a deliberate abandonment by the party leadership.
